Who We Are

Two Generations Of Metal Expertise

DIVI Enterprises is an importer and distributor catering to the mining and stone-processing industry — and to every sector that depends on high-performance wire ropes.

Our Story

Engineered Supply, Built On Trust

We serve the stone cutting, mining, processing and diamond-tool manufacturing sectors with end-to-end solutions — and supply high-performance steel wire ropes to every industry that lifts, hauls and hoists under load.

Our focus spans mining, quarrying, construction, cranes & hoists, material handling and infrastructure. From a 4.9mm diamond-wire-saw rope to a mine-grade hoisting line, every product is selected to match the real demands of your application.

What makes us different is a client-first approach: we work to become your thought partners, helping optimise operations and delivering 360-degree product support for an unparalleled customer experience.

Operating from Jaipur and New Delhi, DIVI Enterprises is an authorized distributor of Asahi Ropes and Bekaert Wires — bringing globally trusted wire and rope brands to Indian industry.
